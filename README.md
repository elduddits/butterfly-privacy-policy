# Privacy Policy for Butterfly – Subscription Tracker

*Last updated: March 31, 2026*

## Overview

Butterfly is designed with your privacy in mind. All data you enter into the app stays on your device and is never collected, shared, or transmitted anywhere.

## Data Collection

Butterfly does not collect any personal data. We do not have access to your name, email address, financial information, or any subscription data you enter into the app.

## Data Storage

All data entered into Butterfly — including your subscriptions, groups, and preferences — is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

## Third Parties

Butterfly does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. Your data is never shared with or sold to third parties.

## Screen Time & Device Activity

Butterfly uses Apple's Screen Time API solely to display app usage information within the app. This data never leaves your device.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be made available within the app and on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the Butterfly Subscription Tracker app, please leave a review in the App Store and I'll gladly take a look.
