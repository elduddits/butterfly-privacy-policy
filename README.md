# Privacy Policy for Butterfly – Subscription Tracker
*Last updated: March 31, 2026*

## Overview
Butterfly is designed with your privacy in mind. All data you enter into the app stays on your device and is never collected, shared, or transmitted anywhere.

## Data Collection
Butterfly does not collect any personal data. We do not have access to your name, email address, financial information, or any subscription data you enter into the app.

## Data Storage
All data entered into Butterfly — including your subscriptions, groups, and preferences — is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

## iCloud Sync
Butterfly uses Apple's iCloud to sync your data across your personal devices. This sync is handled entirely by Apple and is tied to your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data at any time. You can manage or disable iCloud sync at any time in your device's Settings under your Apple ID.

## Third Parties
Butterfly does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. Your data is never shared with or sold to third parties.

## Changes to This Policy
If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be made available within the app and on this page.

# Butterfly – Support
Thanks for using Butterfly! If you're experiencing an issue or have a question, here are a few ways to get help.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### **Is Butterfly really free?**
Yes, completely. No subscriptions, no ads, or anything.

### **My data isn't syncing across devices. What should I do?**
Make sure iCloud is enabled for Butterfly on all your iOS devices under Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Butterfly.

### **The total cost per month for my subscriptions is not adding up**
The cost only considers subscriptiosn that have not been marked as cancelled. This means expiring subscriptiosn will also be excluded from the cost calculation.

### **How do I delete a group?**
Swipe left on a group, then select Delete. Alternatively, you can tap on the Manage Groups button (Folder with Gear) at the top of the home screen. Tap on Edit, then select any group you wish to remove. Tap Delete and choose what to do with any subscription inside the group.
